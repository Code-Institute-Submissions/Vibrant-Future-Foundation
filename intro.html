About Us

Founded in 2016 by Four passionate immigrants in the United Kingdom, we aim to reach out and help people and communities in need. Targeting the children and elders who are the most vulnerable groups in under-developing countries. With your support, we believe that we can share happiness with them, share the same passion towards the wellness of human beings in this world and would like to make further impacts to other people wellbeings in Nepal and worldwide. Currently Charity is operated in United Kingdom and Nepal

Our Coverage

Our Story

It was an evening of 16th June 2016 and we were gathered in Hampstead park in North London for social sports as usual to play football and cricket sports after we finished our work. After few hours of sport, we were just having some relaxed time and friendly conversation in the park. Somehow our conversation directed towards very different topics and we started talking about the people’s life and difficulties in the under-developing countries around the world. It was almost 9 PM and after almost 3 hour-long conversation about the people and lifestyle of Nepal and other under-developed countries around the world, we had many opinions and arguments, finally we all came to the conclusion that indeed there are many people in those countries who desperately need help and support to lift their lives as well as homeless, people who live in poverty and illness. We all have heard and even seen about the life stories of those people on TV, radio or newspapers, but it seems that very minimum action taken to support, hundreds and thousands of funds collect as the name of charity and directly and indirectly many charity organisation have been working in various places around the world but surprisingly peoples were always there on the street and their situation is really devastating.
All of us had spent our early life in Nepal and we have closely seen thousands of people on daily basis who were just struggling to survive, most of them were either disabled or children/elderly and orphan. Of Course, it is not anyone’s responsibility to support them who are in real need but as a human being we found this as an incredibly sad and emotional thing to see people in such a bad situation.
We then start thinking about how we can support those who are in real need situation, finally we conclude to involve ourselves in helping them in whichever way we can and whatever it takes for us to do so, we all had responsibility towards own life, bills and family loan to pay therefore it was very essential for us to work as much as we can and earn money however we strongly agreed that if we can cut down any small expense from ourselves and do some extra work to contribute towards the charity.
we were very determined and commited on this since then so as a initial step we decided to contribute £40 every months from our own pocket and established the charity fund.
Our passion of ‘pay it forward’ leads us to crunch our brains together to perform act of kindness, show generosity and helping the communities and societies they live in. The religions believe by us have taught us about Laws of Karma and we are reaching out to perform the positive actions and thoughts on our daily lives. This Foundation is established as medium to facilitate the charitable donations and volunteering activities lead by us to provide various supports to the targeted groups of vulnerable people within the societies and communities. In addition, friends and colleagues of the us are connected and collaborate together to provide mouth-to-mouth publicities and allowing wider communities to donate and support through this Foundation.
The logo of foundation was carefully designed with ideas contribution from all of us to meet the purposes and goals of this Foundation. Stars in the logo symbolized divine force or energy which represent the main purpose of this Foundation to bring positive vibes to the people-in-need. Rainbow is the sign of hope and faith in various believes and this Foundation is trying to rise up the solidarity feelings among the people to perform act of kindness everywhere. The hand is displaying humble backgrounds of us and our nature to always ready for lending our helping hands. The main colour of this Foundation is green which symbolizes harmony, growth and relaxation which lead towards strong emotional correspondence with safety which we are trying to give and share with the vulnerable people.
