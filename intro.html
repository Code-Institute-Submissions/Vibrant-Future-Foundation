About Us

Founded in 2016 by Four passionate immigrants in the United Kingdom, we aim to reach out and help people and communities in need. Targeting the children and elders who are the most vulnerable groups in under-developing countries. With your support, we believe that we can share happiness with them, share the same passion towards the wellness of human beings in this world and would like to make further impacts to other people wellbeings in Nepal and worldwide. Currently Charity is operated in United Kingdom and Nepal

Our Coverage

We love what we do and passionate about it, the support and aid we give have touched and changed various lives. The differences we make have reached throughout several under-developed countries in Asia and Africa.

Our Target

We focus on social issues and wellbeing of people in communities and societies they live in. Elders and children are categorized as vulnerable age groups who require vast ranges of support. With your helps, we can provide better world for them to live.

Our Approach

We deal with the essential problems and real cases experienced by humanbeings where it is a matter life or death. Roll our sleeves, jump and dive directly to solve the problems, provide solutions and any supports for the people-in-need.
