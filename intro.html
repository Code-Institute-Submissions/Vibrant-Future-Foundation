About Us

Founded in 2016 by Four passionate immigrants in the United Kingdom, we aim to reach out and help people and communities in need. Targeting the children and elders who are the most vulnerable groups in the underdeveloped countries. With your support, we believe that we can share happiness with them, share the same passion towards the wellness of human beings in this world and would like to make further impacts to other people wellbeings in Nepal and worldwide. Currently Charity is operated in the United Kingdom and Nepal.

Our Story

Our Journey began on 16th June 2016 when the four of us gathered in Hampstead Park in North London for social sports as usual to play football and cricket after we finished our work. After few hours of doing sport, we had some relax time and friendly conversation in the park. Somehow our conversation directed towards very different topics and we started talking about the people’s lives and difficulties in the underdeveloped countries around the world. It was almost 9 PM and after almost 3 hour-long conversation about the people and lifestyle in Nepal and other underdeveloped countries around the world, we had many opinions and arguments. Finally, we all came to the conclusion that indeed there are many people in those countries who desperately need help and support to lift up their lives, people who live in poverty, illness and even homeless communities.

All of us had spent our early life in Nepal and we had closely seen thousands of people on daily basis who struggled to survive, most of them were either disabled, children, elderly and orphan. Of course, we can argue that it is not our responsibility to support them however as human being with emotions and feelings, we found these facts incredibly sad and devastating to see people in such a bad situation.

We then started thinking and came up with various plans on how we can support and help those who are in-need, finally we concluded to involve ourselves in helping them in whichever way we can think or execute and whatever it takes for us to do so, we all had responsibility towards our own lives, bills and family loan to pay therefore it was very essential for us to work as much as we can and earn money however, we strongly agreed that we can cut down various small expenses from ourselves and do some extra works to contribute towards the Foundation.

We were very determined and committed to this Foundation and as our very initial step, we decided to contribute £40 every month from our own pockets and established the charity fund which now well known with 'FUND AS WE GO' service.

Our passion of ‘pay it forward’ leads us to crunch our brains together to perform act of kindness, show generosity and helping the communities and societies we live in. Our religions or beliefs have taught us about Laws of Karma and we are reaching out to perform the positive actions and thoughts on our daily lives. This Foundation is established as medium to facilitate the charitable donations and volunteering activities lead by us to provide various supports to the targeted groups of vulnerable people within the societies and communities. In addition, our friends and colleagues are connected and collaborate together to provide mouth-to-mouth publicities which allow wider communities to donate and support through this Foundation.

The logo of the Foundation was carefully designed with ideas contributed from all of us to meet the purposes and goals of this Foundation. Stars in the logo symbolized divine force or energy which represent the main purpose of this Foundation to bring positive vibes to the people-in-need. Rainbow is the sign of hope and faith in various beliefs and this Foundation is trying to rise up the solidarity feelings among the people to perform act of kindness everywhere. The hand is displaying humble backgrounds of us and our nature to always ready for lending our helping hands. The main colour of this Foundation is green which symbolizes harmony, growth and relaxation which lead towards strong emotional correspondence with safety which we are trying to provide and share with the vulnerable people.
