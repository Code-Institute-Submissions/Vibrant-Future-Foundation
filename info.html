Small Steps, Big Impacts

The Founders believe on the philosophy of "The starting point of all achievements is desire." The act of kindness and contribution no matter how small nor significant can lead to broader and wider targets as well as able to reach out even to the most remote areas where people live vulnerably and in dangers. Emphasising on these critical social issues and cases, such as poverty, illness, disability, and education, help the Founders focus on tackling the matters accurately.
The support, aid and helps we provided are made to happen by various interesting and enjoyable activities in life. In addition to these, we are grateful and appreciate the trust given by the communities and societies where it allows the Founders to keep pursuing their dreams and desires to continue make differences and impacts to the lives of the identified vulnerable people.

Vivacious Running

Enjoyment of physical wellbeing, strength, resilient are important aspects for every living organisms.
We value a great benefits of vivacious physical movements which would contribute on wholesome of mental wellbeing.
Running in modern culture sees as sense of progression and race for better life in general.

Super Cycling

Encompass ourselves with mother nature and healthy environment is fundamental part of human welfare.
We feel responsible to contribute towards reduction of ecological footprint and stopping global warming.
Cycling is considered to be one of the best exercise for health as well as pollutions and stress reduction.

Fund as We Go

All the little helps accumulate towards bigger impacts and wider target groups.
The term we introduce here is the service we proudly dedicate it to our targets, the vulnerable people.
Fund as We Go is our way to ensure visibility and transparency of our support and aids reach the appropriate people-in-need.

Mission

We do sport.
We raise donation.
We share it all to make differences.
We advocate for the vulnerable people.
We stand by to let the voices to be heard.
We educate communities and societies about common issues of poverty and hunger.

Vision

To create the world less intimidating for vulnerable age groups.
To transform the world into happier place for children to grow up and elders to live.
